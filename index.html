Couple's Language Learning Tips

1. Daily Practice Together

Set aside 15-30 minutes each day to practice with each other. Consistency is key!

2. Label Items at Home

Place sticky notes with words in both Finnish and Greek on items around your home.

3. Language Date Nights

Choose one evening per week where you only speak in your target languages.

4. Cook Together

Follow recipes in your target languages and learn food-related vocabulary.
