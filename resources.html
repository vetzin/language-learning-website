Create Your Own Resources

As a couple learning each other's languages, you have the unique opportunity to create personalized learning materials for each other.

Custom Flashcards

Create flashcards with words and phrases relevant to your lives together. Include pictures of your actual home, favorite places, and activities.

Voice Recordings

Record native pronunciation of important phrases for each other to practice listening and speaking.

Memory Scrapbook

Create a bilingual scrapbook with photos from your experiences together, including captions in both Finnish and Greek.
