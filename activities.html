Learning Activities for Couples

Learning a language together creates a special bond. Try these activities designed specifically for couples to make language learning fun and effective!

Label Your Home

Place sticky notes with words in Finnish and Greek on objects around your home. Try to use each other's language when referring to these objects.

Beginner 15 min setup

How to do it:

  1. Choose 20 common items in your home
  2. Write each item's name in both Finnish and Greek
  3. Add the transliteration for the Greek words
  4. Use the terms when referring to these objects

Cooking Challenge

Cook a traditional Finnish or Greek dish together using a recipe in the target language. One person reads instructions while the other follows them.

Intermediate 1-2 hours

How to do it:

  1. Find a simple recipe in Finnish or Greek
  2. Gather all ingredients together
  3. Read the instructions aloud in the target language
  4. Try to converse only in the target language during cooking

Word Association Game

Take turns saying a word in your target language. Your partner must respond with a related word. See how long you can keep the chain going!

Beginner 10-15 minutes

How to do it:

  1. First person says a word in their target language
  2. Second person says a related word in their target language
  3. Continue until you can't think of related words
  4. Track your longest chain and try to beat it next time

Daily Text Exchange

Send each other at least one text message every day in your target language. Respond in your partner's target language.

Beginner to Advanced 5 minutes daily

How to do it:

  1. Set a reminder to send a daily text in your target language
  2. Start with simple phrases and gradually increase difficulty
  3. Try to use new vocabulary from the phrases section
  4. Correct each other gently when needed

Movie Night with Subtitles

Watch a Finnish or Greek movie with English subtitles, or an English movie with Finnish or Greek subtitles. Discuss the film afterward.

Intermediate 2 hours

How to do it:

  1. Find a movie in Finnish or Greek (or with those subtitles)
  2. Write down 5 new words/phrases you hear during the movie
  3. Pause occasionally to discuss what's happening
  4. After the movie, try to summarize the plot in simple terms in your target language

Role Play Scenarios

Act out common scenarios like ordering at a restaurant, asking for directions, or shopping - using only your target languages.

Intermediate 15-20 minutes

How to do it:

  1. Choose a common scenario (restaurant, store, etc.)
  2. Research relevant vocabulary beforehand
  3. Act out the scenario in your target languages
  4. Switch roles and repeat for practice

Track Your Progress Together

Set a goal to try at least one activity per week. Keep track of which activities you've completed and which words or phrases you've learned from each one.

Pro Tip

Create a shared note or document where you both add new words and phrases you've learned. Review this list together once a week.
